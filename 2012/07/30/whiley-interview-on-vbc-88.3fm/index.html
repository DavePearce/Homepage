Whiley Interview on VBC 88.3FM

Monday, July 30th, 2012

Recently, I was on VBC 88.3FM (Victoria University’s Student Radio) giving a 60s “speed summary” of my research, and generally chatting about some tech stuff.  The station has been interviewing a whole range of people at the university to raise awareness of what research is going on.  Anyhow, I agreed to do the interview which was fun — and here’s the MP3.

Anyway, the interview is pretty short and doesn’t cover a lot of detail.  But, it was kind of fun being on the radio! I think the question about what food I’d eat if I could eat anything was the hardest … and I should have known better than to say “Linux Operating System”.  Still, it was good to practice my “pitch” …

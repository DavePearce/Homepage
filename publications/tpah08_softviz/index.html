Tutte Polynomials
Tutte polynomials play an import role in graph theory, combinatorics, matroid theory, knot theory, and experimental physics. This project involved developing a high efficient algorithm (implemented in C++) for computing Tutte polynomials which was later incorporated into Mathematic and Sage.
